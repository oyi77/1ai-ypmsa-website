## CONTENT PRODUCTION NOTES

**Equipment Needed**:
- Smartphone (min. 12MP camera)
- Tripod or gimbal stabilizer
- Lavalier mic (clip-on)
- Ring light for indoor shots
- Editing app: CapCut (free)

**Filming Best Practices**:
- Vertical format (9:16 ratio)
- Natural lighting preferred
- Show REAL students (with parental consent)
- Avoid stock footage - use authentic footage
- Include subtitles (85% watch without sound)
- Keep text on screen >1 second per word

**Legal Compliance**:
- Get written consent for student photos/videos
- Blur faces if consent unavailable
- Never show full names of minors
- Include disclaimer: "Nama disamarkan untuk privasi"
- Store consent forms (digital + physical)
